## Final strategic position

The vendor swipes confirm that the offer uses aggressive wealth, conspiracy,
neuroscience, health, urgency, and testimonial-based persuasion.

Our affiliate funnel must not imitate that tone.

Our funnel must create a trust buffer through:

- original educational content
- a separate brand
- a standalone lead magnet
- transparent disclosures
- an independent review page
- attributed vendor claims
- no unverified financial or health claims
- no fake urgency
- no copied testimonials
- no unauthorized imagery

The preferred structure is now:

Morning Mindset Reset
→ The 7-Minute Morning Clarity Reset
→ trust-first email nurture
→ Billionaire Brain Wave Review page
→ vendor presentation
