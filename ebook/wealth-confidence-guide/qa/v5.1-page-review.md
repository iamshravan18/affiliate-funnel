## Visual-QA method (honest note)

All 25 pages were **rendered** to PNG at 1.4× and are available for inspection.
Pages **2, 20, and 21** were visually inspected directly (high-resolution crops
/ full-page renders) to confirm the citation gluing, the five card icons, and the
new page-21 field with its bottom margin. The remaining pages were verified
**programmatically** from the PDF text layer, link annotations, drawing/geometry
positions, font descriptors, and the metadata/bookmark structure — not each
eyeballed individually. No automated check surfaced a layout regression.

## Known limitations

- EPUB and HTML do **not** reproduce the native Typst worksheet grids, cards, or
  the references page layout; the **PDF is authoritative**.
- Publisher DOI links return 403 to automated checkers (anti-bot); they resolve
  normally in a browser.
