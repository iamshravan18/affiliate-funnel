# Image Manifest — The Wealth Confidence Guide

**Status:** All entries are **branded placeholders** in this phase. Final visual
assets are produced separately (RULE 9). Placeholders are dimensionally correct
so layout and page flow are accurate now. Do **not** stretch low-resolution
website images into these print slots.

**Global rules for every asset**
- Palette only: Deep Green `#0F3D2E`, Evergreen `#0B241F`, Warm Ivory `#F7F3E9`,
  Soft Gold `#C9A15B` (sparingly), Sage `#A7BFA3`, Warm Sand `#E6D8C3`,
  Taupe `#D6CDB8`.
- Fonts in art: Cormorant Garamond (display) + Manrope (labels).
- Mood: calm, premium, editorial, grounded, optimistic, mature.
- Grayscale-safe: must read clearly with no color.
- **Prohibited in all art:** cash piles, gold coins, crypto/trading/stock charts,
  mansions, luxury cars, glowing brains, neuroscience diagrams, mystical/energy
  or manifestation graphics, black-and-gold "billionaire" styling, app-dashboard
  UI, fake testimonials, fake signatures, tiny decorative type, heavy shadows.
- Print: 300 DPI. Safe text area = keep text ≥ 0.35in from every edge.

## Detailed briefs

### IMG-01 — Cover
- **Composition:** Portrait cover. Warm ivory ground with a calm deep-green band.
  Centered Cormorant title "The Wealth Confidence Guide"; Manrope subtitle
  "Simple daily money-awareness shifts for calmer financial decisions." Small
  Growth Circle mark. A single restrained gold hairline accent.
- **Objects/people:** None required; optional abstract "growth circle" motif. No
  people.
- **Lighting:** Soft, even, editorial.
- **Exact text:** Title + subtitle + "Micro Saving Daily" + "Small habits.
  Stronger money confidence."
- **Alt text:** "Cover of The Wealth Confidence Guide by Micro Saving Daily, in
  deep green and warm ivory with a small growth-circle mark."
- **Prohibited:** anything in the global prohibited list.

### IMG-02 — Welcome editorial visual
- **Composition:** Wide, airy. A calm still-life feeling: a notebook and pen on a
  warm surface, soft daylight. Suggests reflection, not wealth.
- **Alt text:** "A simple open notebook and pen on a calm, warm-toned surface."
- **Prohibited:** money imagery, luxury.

### IMG-03 … IMG-08 — Framework / day concept visuals (square)
- **IMG-03 Notice:** an eye-like or magnifier-over-circle motif; "seeing clearly."
  Alt: "A simple line motif suggesting noticing one small thing."
- **IMG-04 Pause:** a gentle pause symbol / a held breath / a bench. Alt: "A calm
  line motif suggesting a brief pause."
- **IMG-05 Triggers:** a small map of a moment (place, time, feeling icons). Alt:
  "A simple map motif showing parts of a moment: place, time, and feeling."
- **IMG-06 Shift:** a path gently changing direction. Alt: "A line motif of a path
  making one small, calm turn."
- **IMG-07 Environment:** a tidy shelf / phone set aside. Alt: "A calm motif of a
  phone set face-down beside a short list."
- **IMG-08 Repeat:** the Growth Circle repeating gently. Alt: "A soft repeating
  circle motif suggesting steady repetition."
- **Style (all):** single-color line art in deep green on ivory, gold used only as
  a tiny accent dot. Grayscale-safe.

### IMG-09 — Seven-day progress visual
- **Composition:** A calm horizontal row of seven small circles, gently rising,
  the last one softly filled in gold. No numbers of dollars.
- **Alt text:** "Seven small circles in a gentle rising line, the last softly
  highlighted, suggesting a week of steady practice."

### IMG-10 … IMG-16 — Worksheet header bands
- **Composition:** Thin decorative band above each worksheet with the worksheet
  name in Cormorant and a small Growth Circle mark. Functional, quiet.
- **Alt text (pattern):** "Decorative header band for the [worksheet name]."
- **Note:** The worksheets themselves are rendered as live, fillable layouts in
  the document; these bands are optional visual headers only.

### IMG-17 — Today's Small Shift card
- **Composition:** A small "card" object with the words "Today's Small Shift" in
  Cormorant and one blank line, in brand colors.
- **Alt text:** "A small card reading 'Today's Small Shift' with space for one
  note."

### IMG-18 — Final brand visual
- **Composition:** The Growth Circle mark with the wordmark "Micro Saving Daily"
  and tagline. Calm, centered, generous space.
- **Alt text:** "Micro Saving Daily growth-circle mark with the tagline 'Small
  habits. Stronger money confidence.'"
